History


The Wacombs theological seminary was established in 1997 about 23 years ago in Lagos Nigeria formerly known as Roberts Bible Training Center And Graduating School

it started at the premises of a school named lafred primary and secondary school around iponri Costain surulere,with only 3 student all at certificate level.

During her trying period, she moved into an hotel room, and later went to graduate the first set of student.

The challenge for a permanent and reliable site saw the school moving from one area within Lagos to another

in 2004 the school graduate student at the national stadium with her oldest student by the name elder fowowe of blessed memory, former chairman of Nigeria brewery.

The college currently is located at the Agric axis ikorodu Lagos east senatorial district, on her permanent site
